My professional project

I'm driven to build and optimize large-scale, robust infrastructure systems where software engineering meets systems thinking. I seek environments that value automation, innovation, and solving complex technical problems in high-performance or cloud-native contexts.

Objectives

Master advanced DevOps and infrastructure automation at scale. Deepen expertise in distributed systems, cloud architecture, and AI integration. Transition toward roles with greater architectural responsibility and influence on system design decisions.

Target Role / Function

DevOps Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer (SRE), or Infrastructure Architect roles in organizations building mission-critical or high-scale systems. Roles emphasizing automation, infrastructure-as-code, and system optimization.

Market & Sector

Prefer established tech companies, cloud service providers, or specialized infrastructure firms with strong engineering cultures. Attractive environments include those working on open-source infrastructure, distributed systems, or cutting-edge cloud technologies.

Career Projection

In 3–5 years, I aim to move into a senior infrastructure or systems architecture role where I lead technical decisions on large-scale deployments. I envision specializing in areas like advanced automation, Kubernetes orchestration, or AI system integration; roles where deep technical knowledge translates into significant business impact and mentorship opportunities.
